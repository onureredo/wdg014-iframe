Cauliflower Curry

Easy, rich, creamy and warmly-spiced, this cauliflower-loaded coconut curry redefines comfort food. It’s vegan and gluten free, so it’s a great meal to serve to folks with food sensitivities.

Servings: 4
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 4-6 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, torn into pieces
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Arrange the sliced tomatoes and mozzarella cheese on a platter or individual plates.
  2. Scatter the torn basil leaves over the top of the salad.
  3. Drizzle with the olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
